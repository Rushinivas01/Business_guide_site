  1. Operational Efficiency: IndiGo focuses on maintaining operational efficiency to keep costs under control. They optimize their flight schedules, ground handling processes, and turnaround times to minimize aircraft idle time and maximize utilization.
  2. No-Frills Service: IndiGo offers a no-frills service model, providing customers with a simple and basic flying experience. This approach allows the airline to avoid the high costs associated with premium services and amenities.
  3. Young Fleet: IndiGo maintains a young fleet of fuel-efficient aircraft, primarily consisting of Airbus A320 family planes. A young fleet helps reduce maintenance costs, fuel consumption, and carbon emissions.
  4. Direct Sales and Online Booking: IndiGo encourages direct sales through their website and mobile app, avoiding distribution costs associated with travel agents and third-party booking platforms.
